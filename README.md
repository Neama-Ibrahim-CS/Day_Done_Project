# ✅ Day Done 

A smart, console-based productivity companion designed to help students and high-achievers manage their daily tasks, track their streaks, and maintain a historical archive of their progress.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Reality Planning:** A smart filter that ensures your daily goals match your available hours (No more over-scheduling!).
* **Gamified Experience:** Earn **XP** and level up your **Rank** as you complete tasks.
* **Streak System:** Tracks your consistency and motivates you to keep going every day.
* **Smart Archive:** Saves your daily satisfaction levels and personal messages to a local database (`daydone_archive.txt`).
* **Accumulated Tasks:** Automatically carries over unfinished tasks from previous days as "Bronze Tasks."

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation
* **Language:** C++
* **Concepts Used:** * Object-Oriented Programming (OOP).
    * File Handling (I/O Streams) for data persistence.
    * Data Structures (Vectors, Structs).
    * Algorithms (Custom sorting based on priority).

## 🚀 How it Works
1. Enter your available study hours for the day.
2. Add your tasks with estimated time and priority (1-10).
3. The system performs a **Reality Check** to ensure your plan is doable.
4. Complete tasks to gain XP and maintain your streak.
5. End the day with a "Satisfaction Review" and a message to your future self.
